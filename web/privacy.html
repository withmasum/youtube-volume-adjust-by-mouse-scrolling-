Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the YouTube Volume Control by Mouse Scrolling extension ("the Extension") handles your data. The Extension is designed to respect your privacy and does not collect any personal information.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. It only accesses the YouTube video player on your device to adjust volume levels. The only data stored by the Extension are your chosen settings (such as volume step size) in your browser's local storage.

Data Use

No user data is sent to any servers or third parties. The Extension operates entirely on your device. We do not use any analytics or tracking tools. All adjustments to video volume occur locally in your browser.

Permissions

The Extension requires permission to access YouTube (youtube.com and related domains) in order to function. It does not require any additional permissions (such as personal data or access to other websites). The Extension will only run on YouTube pages and will not affect any other browsing activity.

Data Retention

Your volume preferences and settings are stored locally in the browser so that your chosen settings persist between sessions. No data is retained by us or on any external servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will provide a notice on the extension's page. Continued use of the Extension after changes implies your acceptance of the new policy.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at support@masumnotes.com.
